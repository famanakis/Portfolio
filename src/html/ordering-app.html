Restaurant Ordering App

Live Demo

As a developer, I am excited to showcase my Restaurant Ordering App project on my portfolio website. The app is a solo project that uses HTML, CSS, CSS-Flexbox, and JavaScript to create a user-friendly interface for restaurant customers to place their orders. The project meets the requirements of rendering menu options using JavaScript and allowing users to add and remove items from their order. Additionally, it has a payment modal with compulsory form inputs, making it easy for customers to pay for their order.

As I developed this project, I enjoyed the challenge of following the design spec to ensure that the app had a professional and visually appealing appearance. The screenshots of the Main Menu, Order Totals, and Checkout pages on the project README demonstrate the app's design and functionality. With a simple and easy-to-use interface, customers can quickly place their orders, and the app allows for customization, such as changing the theme and offering a meal deal discount.



I am proud of the finished product and am excited to have it live on my portfolio website. The app's use of JavaScript to render menu options and allow for adding and removing items from an order showcases my skills as a developer. Additionally, the payment modal and stretch goals of allowing users to rate their experience demonstrate my attention to detail and focus on user experience. Overall, the Restaurant Ordering App project is a testament to my abilities as a developer and my commitment to creating high-quality, user-friendly applications.
